Instructions

Instructions


1. Choose a harvesting tool from your tool box.

2. Each tool is used to harvest specific elements.

3. Harvested elements go to your inventory and can be used to creat surprizing new materials.

4. Use the reset button to refresh the map.

5. Use the music buttons to control the volume of the background music.


Pickaxe: mines stone, gold, diampnd, and silver.

Axe: breaks wood and trees, and bushes.

Shovel: picks up dirt, ground, snow, etc.

Hoe: harvests lave and bricks.

Bucket: harvests lave and water.

SWORD!: use to KILL the Legendary END Dragon.


To summon the shortcut keyboard, please press K.

Have fun!

DISCLAIMER: This mini-game was developed in two days, thus it has some bugs, so please be kind
